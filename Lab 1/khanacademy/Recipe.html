Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Egg yolks 4
Parmesan cheese 1 cup
Pancetta 150g

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the egg yolks with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Toss the spaghetti with pancetta, then add the egg mixture while hot.
  5. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan.

Japanese Ramen

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Ramen noodles 2 packs
Chicken broth 4 cups
Soft-boiled eggs 2
Chashu pork 150g

Steps:

  1. Heat chicken broth and add seasoning.
  2. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare toppings: soft-boiled eggs, Chashu pork, and green onions.
  4. Assemble ramen bowls and pour broth over noodles.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 12 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Butter 1/2 cup
Chocolate chips 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Mix in flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop dough onto baking tray and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Cool before serving.
